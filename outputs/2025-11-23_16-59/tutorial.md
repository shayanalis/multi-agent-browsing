# How to how can i create a notion database

**Status:** timeout
**Total Steps:** 19

---

## Instructions

Follow these steps to complete the task:

### Step 2

Click on the 'New Database' button located on the left sidebar to create a new database in Notion.

![Step 2](step_001.png)

*You should see a blank database page where you can start adding your entries.*

---

### Step 3

Click on the button labeled 'New page' to create a new database.

![Step 3](step_002.png)

*After clicking, you should see a new page open where you can start setting up your database.*

---

### Step 4

Click on the 'Database' option in the menu that appears.

![Step 4](step_003.png)

*You should see a new database page open, ready for you to customize.*

---

### Step 5

Click on the 'Empty database' option to create a new database.

![Step 5](step_004.png)

*After this step, you should see a blank database interface ready for you to customize.*

---

### Step 6

Click on the area at coordinates 799, 518 to open the newly created database.

![Step 6](step_005.png)

*You should see the empty database interface ready for you to add content.*

---

### Step 7

Click on the 'Get started' button to proceed with creating your database.

![Step 7](step_006.png)

*After clicking, you should see the setup options for your new database.*

---

### Step 8

Click on the 'Create Database' button to finalize the setup of your new Notion database.

![Step 8](step_007.png)

*You should see a new blank database ready for you to add content and customize.*

---

### Step 9

Click on the 'Done' button to finalize your database setup.

![Step 9](step_008.png)

*After clicking 'Done', you should see your new Notion database ready for use.*

---

### Step 10

Click on the button labeled 'Settings' to access the database settings.

![Step 10](step_009.png)

*You should see the settings menu for your Notion database open up.*

---

### Step 11

Click on the area at coordinates 799, 728 on the screen to proceed.

![Step 11](step_010.png)

*After clicking, you should see the next step of the database creation process.*

---

### Step 12

Click on the 'All Requests' button located in the sidebar menu.

![Step 12](step_011.png)

*After clicking, you should see a list of all requests displayed on the screen.*

---

### Step 13

Click on the 'Requested date' button to view or edit the date associated with the request.

![Step 13](step_012.png)

*After clicking, you should see a date picker or options related to the requested date.*

---

### Step 14

Click on the 'Insert right' button to add a new column to your database.

![Step 14](step_013.png)

*You should see a new column appear on the right side of your database.*

---

### Step 15

In the new column that appears, click on the text field and type 'Notes'.

![Step 15](step_014.png)

*After completing this step, you should see 'Notes' displayed as the title of the new column.*

---

### Step 16

Click on the 'Text' option in the dropdown menu that appears.

![Step 16](step_015.png)

*After completing this step, you should see a text field added to your Notion database.*

---

### Step 17

In the text field that appears, type 'My New Database' and press Enter.

![Step 17](step_016.png)

*You should see 'My New Database' displayed as the title of your new database.*

---

### Step 18

Click on the 'New' button located at the top right of the database page.

![Step 18](step_017.png)

*After clicking, you should see a new entry form appear for adding items to your database.*

---

### Step 19

In the new database entry field, type 'Test Entry 1'.

![Step 19](step_018.png)

*You should see 'Test Entry 1' appear in the database entry field.*
